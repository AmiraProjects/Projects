The Jungle Adventure : Save The Plants

The Jungle Adventure is a platformer game made using Construct3. It's about a boy who was sent by his teacher to save endangered plants and puts them in the botanical garden. There are 3 plants to save. In the middle of a jungle where he must save the plants, there are so many obstacle he must pass. Such as poisonous water/river which is dangerous to drink or fallen in, fragile rocks that makes cliffs harder to climb. All these thing happens because of illegal activities done by humans in the forest.

Feeding Fish

Feeding Fish is an endless game made with Construct3. In this game, players must feed the fish with small fishes deep under the ocean just like the game title. However, there is so many trashes which was thrown in the sea, sinks near the hungry fish area. That makes it so much harder to eat small fishes as many of them swim away. Other than that, the longer you play, the more dangerous sea creatures that came like octopuses, sharks, jellyfish. Those creatures also wants food because their area was full of trashes.

Here's the link to my project Feeding Fish

The Escape Game

The Escape Game is also made with Construct3, it is a sci-fi themed platformer game where you play as an alien who was captured. The alien must find its way to escape the prison and find its friend in the middle of no where. After it meet its friend, it must continue its way to the nearest town along with its friend. However, in its way on finding its friend and going to the town, there was many enemies it founded who hunted the alien and wants to bring it back to the prison. Additionally, the hunters set so many traps that the alien must avoided.
